Making a positive change, one bike at a time!

If you are a busy person looking for a climate friendly, effective and yet cheap way to get around, we have the sollution for you!

Using bikeshare, you will be able to both access other bikes in your area, and lend your own bike out to people around you.

Be the change

Bikeshare is the optimal way of getting around fast without having a bad effect on the climate. Instead of using loud and not so climate-friendly cars, you can lead the world in a better direction by utilizing a bike.

Stay healthy

Biking around instead of just sitting still in a car or on a bus, is not only good for the climate. It is good for you as well!
Stay healthy and fit by using BikeShare everyday.
